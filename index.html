STORY
It was the age of warring states.

Across treacherous valeys and moiuntain ridges thick with snow lay the land of Ashina. From the nothern proviance came lagendary swordsman, Isshin Ashina, who wrested control and restored glory to these lands in the passing of a single generation. However, after a time Ashina was met with a fateful crisis.


A young commander of Ashina's forces, Isshin's own grandson was distraught at the plight of his grandfather's realm. Gathering his most trusted troops, he devised a covert operation. "No longer can we hope to defend ourselves by conventional means. We must act now. Bring the Divine Heir to me."

Thus, the Divine Heir was taken prisoner. Heir by title andnothing more, the young boy had no living relatives; no family nor dependentsto his name. Save for a lone shinobi...

This is the tale of a lord and his retainer, a forlorn pair with nowhere else to turn.
